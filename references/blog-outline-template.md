# Blog Outline Template

When generating a blog outline, always follow this structure:

## Suggested Title
Use plain language. Include a number or benefit where possible.
Example: "5 Ways to Cut Azure VM Costs Without Changing Your Architecture"

## Meta
- Target audience: [e.g. Cloud architects, FinOps practitioners, IT managers]
- Estimated read time: [5–8 min recommended]
- Primary Azure offer to feature: [pick from azure-pricing-content.md]
- Community pain point: [pulled from scraping-targets sources]

## Outline

### Intro (1 paragraph)
- Hook: open with the community concern or pain point
- Why it matters now
- What the reader will learn

### Section 1 — The Problem
- What are people getting wrong or struggling with?
- Use 1 real community example (Reddit/Tech Community thread)

### Section 2 — The Official Microsoft Approach
- Pull official wording from official-microsoft-sources.md
- Explain the relevant Azure offer clearly
- Include the key savings stat

### Section 3 — How It Works (Step-by-Step)
- 3–5 practical steps
- Link to official docs

### Section 4 — Real-World Example or Scenario
- A relatable customer scenario
- Before vs After comparison (cost or complexity)

### Conclusion
- Recap the key benefit
- Call to Action: link to Pricing Calculator or relevant offer page

[suggest 5–7 based on the topic and community language]
